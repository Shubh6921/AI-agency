Aether Finance

Automated customer support for global fintech leaders.

Client: Aether Group Industry: Fintech Services: AI Automation, Product Design
✦ THE CHALLENGE

Aether Group faced a critical bottleneck: scaling their customer support infrastructure without compromising the elite, white-glove service expected by their high-net-worth clientele. Their existing manual workflows were failing to keep pace with a 300% surge in user acquisition, leading to latency in response times and increased operational friction.

✦ THE APPROACH

Our engineering scope focused on the integration of a proprietary LLM-driven automation engine. We redesigned the agent interface from the ground up, prioritizing cognitive load reduction and structural clarity. The strategy moved away from reactive ticketing toward a predictive workspace that suggests resolutions before agents even engage.

✦ THE OUTCOME

The resulting system transformed Aether's operational model. By automating 82% of routine inquiries with high precision, the support team was liberated to focus exclusively on complex advisory roles. The new interface reduced onboarding time for new agents by 50% while significantly boosting customer satisfaction scores through near-instantaneous response loops.
